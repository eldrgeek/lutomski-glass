The Craftsman

Forty Years of Light

Bill Lutomski has been transforming sunlight into art since 1982 — a career spanning four decades, thousands of original pieces, and a lineage that traces directly to the Prairie masters.

Trained by the great-granddaughter of Frank Lloyd Wright, Bill carries forward the organic geometry and reverence for natural light that defined an American architectural movement. From his Denver studio, he designs and fabricates every panel by hand — cutting, leading, and soldering with the same patient attention that has defined his craft since the Reagan era.

His work graces private homes, historic churches, restaurants, casinos, government buildings, libraries, and corporate campuses across Colorado and beyond. Whether a single decorative accent or a floor-to-ceiling statement wall, each piece begins the same way: with Bill at a light table, tracing lines that will one day sing with color.

What We Do

Services

From a single accent panel to a grand installation, every project receives the same meticulous craft.

Custom Design

Original stained glass windows designed around your vision — architectural style, color palette, subject matter, and space. Every piece is one-of-a-kind.

  • Residential windows & doors
  • Art Deco & geometric panels
  • Prairie & mid-century modern
  • Figurative & landscape designs

Restoration & Repair

Expert conservation and restoration of antique and damaged stained glass. Broken panes, failed leading, sagging panels — brought back to their original brilliance.

  • Lead came replacement
  • Broken glass repair
  • Historic window conservation
  • Church & chapel restoration

Installation

Professional installation in new and existing frames. Bill handles the full process — fabrication, framing, and finish — ensuring every panel is perfectly seated and structurally sound.

  • New window installation
  • Panel re-hanging & resetting
  • Custom framing & zinc channels
  • Exterior protective glazing

Commercial Projects

Large-scale commissions for restaurants, hotels, casinos, government buildings, libraries, and corporate spaces. Experienced in complex multi-panel installations with strict deadlines.

  • Restaurants & hospitality
  • Government & civic buildings
  • Corporate lobbies & atriums
  • Religious institutions
